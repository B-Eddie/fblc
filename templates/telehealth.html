Telehealth Services

Available Telehealth Options

Connect with healthcare providers from the comfort of your home:

  • Video consultations with doctors
  • Mental health therapy sessions
  • Follow-up appointments
  • Prescription renewals

How It Works

  1. Choose a healthcare provider
  2. Select an available time slot
  3. Receive a confirmation email with link
  4. Join the video call at the scheduled time
  5. Discuss your health concerns with the provider
  6. Get prescriptions or follow-up instructions

Benefits of Telehealth

  • Convenient access to care
  • Reduced travel time and costs
  • Minimized exposure to illnesses
  • Access to specialists outside your area
  • Flexible scheduling options
